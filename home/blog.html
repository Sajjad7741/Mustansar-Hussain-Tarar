Guzara Nahi Hota Impact On Reader..!

posted by RedPrince007 | Read Full... | 12 comments.

Guzara Nahi Hota Urdu book is one of The masterpiece Of Mustansar Hussain Tarar. This Urdu book is about fun, comedy and jokes. In this book he has collected many Urdu stories those will make you laugh and smile. Guzara Nahi Hota Urdu book is all about entertainment.

In All Of The Chapters There Are Many Lessons, Facts That We Miss In Our Daily Life; As A Nation, As A Person, And As A Human Being. The Message Is Passed In Such A Way That Reader Do Not Get Bored, Laughs On Each Lines And Realize As This Was Also Done With Him.
